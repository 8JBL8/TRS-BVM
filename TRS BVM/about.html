About Us

A way to predict future is TO INVENT IT
learn | explore | innovate

Since July 2020, TRS BVM student chapter is a platform for students to hone their problem-solving skills through competitions and constructively working on real-time projects and making it big in the world of Robotics and Automation. The chapter is attuned to help students to get the most out of their experience while they are enrolled also for their academics. It greatly helps students in learning to work as a team which is an essential skill for students’ career.

As major activities of the chapter, students make their own robots, write their own code, and do their own crazy stuff. The chapter is very well supported by a team of coherent yet innovative minds and accomplished professors who have in-depth experience in various disciplines of robotics. The chapter encourages each member to convert an individual's idea into a product. The chapter provides an environment to participate in State-level, National-level and International level competitions, hackathons, etc. through 24X7 laboratories which are equipped with electronic instruments, mechanical equipment/machines, 3D printers, computer systems and many more.

The chapter also organizes workshops, expert lectures, boot-camps, seminars, etc. to enhance skills of students/faculties. The chapter has registered more than 200 students/faculty members till date.

“An opportunity to THINK, DESIGN, AND CREATE! Do more at TRS BVM!”

Objectives

TRS BVM Student Chapter promotes the shaping of skills of the students to make them life-ready for working out real-life science problems.

Inspiration
To promote interest, educate and provide hands-on experience in the field of robotics and engineering.
Innovation
To develop innovative solutions for demanding problems.
Expression
To encourage each individual to put forward their ideas and convert into a product.
Empowerment
To enhance skills of members in emerging technologies like Machine Learning, Mechanical Design, Electronics, IoT, Networks, and Data Science.
Community
To build a system full of enthusiastic engineering minds that encourage student members to dig into MIND through events and competitions.
